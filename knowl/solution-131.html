Solution 2.3.14.1.

  1. Using the Power and Constant Multiple Rules, we have: \(\fp(x) = 8x\text{.}\) Continuing on, we have

    \begin{align*} \fp'(x)\amp=\lzoo{x}{8x}=8\amp\fp''(x)\amp=0\amp f^{(4)}(x)\amp=0\text{.} \end{align*}

    Notice how all successive derivatives will also be \(0\text{.}\)

  2. We employ Theorem 2.3.1 repeatedly.

    \begin{align*} \fp(x)\amp=\cos(x)\amp \fp''(x)\amp= -\cos(x)\\ \fp'(x)\amp= -\sin(x)\amp f^{(4)}(x)\amp = \sin(x) \end{align*}

    Note how we have come right back to \(f(x)\) again. (Can you quickly figure what \(f^{(23)}(x)\) is?)

  3. Employing Theorem 2.3.1 and the Constant Multiple Rule, we can see that

    \begin{equation*} \fp(x) = \fp'(x) = \fp''(x) = f^{(4)}(x) = 5e^x\text{.} \end{equation*}
