Another method that can be more accurate than the Trapezoidal Rule is the Midpoint Rule:

\begin{align*} S_M(n)\amp =\sum_{i=1}^n f\left(\frac{x_{i-1}+x_{i}}{2}\right)\Delta x\\ \amp = \sum_{i=1}^n f\left(\overline{x_i}\right)\Delta x\\ \amp \text{ where } \overline{x_i} \text{ is the midpoint of each subinterval,}\\ \amp \overline{x_i}=a+\dx\left(i-\frac12\right) \end{align*}
