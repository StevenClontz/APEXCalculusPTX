\(\frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2}-\frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2}=1\text{;}\) hyperbola centered at \((h,k)\) with horizontal transverse axis and asymptotes with slope \(b/a\text{.}\) The parametric equations only give half of the hyperbola. When \(a>0\text{,}\) the right half; when \(a\lt 0\text{,}\) the left half.
