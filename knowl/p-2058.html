  1. Applying the Product Rule:

    \begin{align*} \lzoo{x}{x\ln(x)}\amp= 1\cdot \ln(x) + x\cdot 1/x\\ \amp = \ln(x) + 1\text{.} \end{align*}

  2. Using the result from above, we compute

    \begin{align*} \lzoo{x}{x\ln(x) -x}\amp= \ln(x) + 1 - 1\\ \amp= \ln(x)\text{.} \end{align*}
