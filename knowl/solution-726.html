\(f^{(n)}(x) = (-1)^ne^{-x}\text{;}\) at \(x=0\text{,}\) \(f^{(n)}(0)=-1\) when \(n\) is odd and \(f^{(n)}(0)=1\) when \(n\) is even.

The Taylor series starts \(1-x+\frac12x^2-\frac1{3!}x^3+\cdots\text{;}\)

the Taylor series is \(\ds \infser[0] (-1)^n\frac{x^n}{n!}\text{.}\)
