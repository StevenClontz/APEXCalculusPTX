Theorem 15.5.22. Surface Area of Parametrically Defined Surfaces.

Let \(\vec r(u,v)\) be a smooth parametrization of a surface \(\surfaceS\) over a closed, bounded region \(R\) of the \(u\)-\(v\) plane.

  • The surface area differential \(dS\) is: \(dS = \snorm{\vec r_u\times \vec r_v}\, dA\text{.}\)

  • The surface area \(S\) of \(\surfaceS\) is

    \begin{equation*} S = \iint_\surfaceS\, dS = \iint_R \snorm{\vec r_u\times \vec r_v}\, dA\text{.} \end{equation*}
