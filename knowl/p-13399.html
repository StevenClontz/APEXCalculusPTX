Approximating \(4.1\) with 4 gives \(dx = 0.1\text{;}\) approximating \(0.8\) with \(\pi/4\) gives \(dy \approx 0.015\text{.}\) Thus

\begin{align*} dz(4,\pi/4) \amp = f_x(4,\pi/4)(0.1) + f_y(4,\pi/4)(0.015)\\ \amp = \frac{\sqrt{2}}8(0.1) + \sqrt{2}(0.015)\\ \amp \approx 0.039\text{.} \end{align*}
