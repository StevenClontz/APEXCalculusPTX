Using the constant and quotient rules,

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \lim\limits_{x\to 9}\left(\frac{3f(x)}{g(x)}\right)\amp = \frac{\lim\limits_{x\to 9}(3f(x))}{\lim\limits_{x\to 9}g(x)}\\ \amp = \frac{3\lim\limits_{x\to 9}f(x)}{\lim\limits_{x\to 9}g(x)} \\ \amp \frac{3(6)}{3} = 6 \text{.} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
