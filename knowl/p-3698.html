\begin{equation*} \lz{B}{t} = \frac{C\lz{C}{t}- A\lz{A}{t}}{B}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Now we substitue in our known rates and instantaneous values of our variables:

\begin{align*} \lz{B}{t} \amp \approx \frac{0.707(20)- 0.5(-30)}{(0.5)}\\ \amp= 58.28 \text{ mph }\text{.} \end{align*}
