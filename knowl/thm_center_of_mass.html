Theorem 14.4.16. Center of Mass of a Planar Lamina, Moments.

Let a planar lamina be represented by a closed, bounded region \(R\) in the \(xy\)-plane with density function \(\delta(x,y)\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \ds \text{ mass: } M = \iint_R\delta(x,y)\, dA\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \ds \text{ moment about the \(x\)-axis: } M_x = \iint_Ry\delta(x,y)\, dA\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \ds \text{ moment about the \(y\)-axis: } M_y = \iint_Rx\delta(x,y)\, dA\)

  4. The center of mass of the lamina is

    \begin{equation*} (\overline{x},\overline{y}) = \left(\frac{M_y}{M},\frac{M_x}M\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}
