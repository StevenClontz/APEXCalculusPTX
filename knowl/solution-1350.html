\(\ds\int_2^4\int_{x-1}^{7-x}\, dy\, dx\text{.}\) The order \(dx\, dy\) needs two iterated integrals as \(x\) is bounded above by two different functions. This gives:

\begin{equation*} \int_{1}^3\int_{2}^{y+1}\, dx\, dy+\int_{3}^5\int_{2}^{7-y}\, dx\, dy\text{.} \end{equation*}
