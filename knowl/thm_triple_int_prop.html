Theorem 14.6.19. Properties of Triple Integrals.

Let \(D\) be a closed, bounded region in space, and let \(D_1\) and \(D_2\) be non-overlapping regions such that \(D=D_1\bigcup D_2\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \ds \iiint_D \, dV \geq 0\)

  2. \(\ds \iiint_D\, dV = \iiint_{D_1}\, dV + \iiint_{D_2}\, dV\text{.}\)
