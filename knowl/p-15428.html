Let \(C\) be a smooth curve parametrized by the arc-length parameter \(s\text{,}\) let \(f\) and \(g\) be continuous functions of \(s\text{,}\) and let \(k_1\) and \(k_2\) be scalars. Then

\begin{equation*} \ds \int_C\big(k_1f(s)+k_2g(s)\big)\, ds = k_1\int_Cf(s)\, ds +k_2\int_Cg(s)\, ds\text{.} \end{equation*}
