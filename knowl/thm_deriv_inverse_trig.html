Theorem 2.7.14. Derivatives of Inverse Trigonometric Functions.

The inverse trigonometric functions are differentiable on all open sets contained in their domains (as listed in TableÂ 2.7.13) and their derivatives are as follows:

  1. \(\displaystyle \lzoo{x}{\sin^{-1}(x)} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \lzoo{x}{\cos^{-1}(x)} = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\)
