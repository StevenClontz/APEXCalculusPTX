It appears that when \(x\) is close to \(0\text{,}\) that \({\frac{x-5}{x^{2}-4x}}\) grows without bound. So \(\lim_{x\to 0}\left({\frac{x-5}{x^{2}-4x}}\right)\) does not exist (DNE).
