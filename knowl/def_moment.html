Definition 14.4.12. Moments about the \(x\) and \(y\) Axes.

Let point masses \(m_1\text{,}\) \(m_2,\ldots,m_n\) be located at points

\begin{equation*} (x_1,y_1), (x_2,y_2), \ldots, (x_n,y_n)\text{,} \end{equation*}

respectively, in the \(xy\)-plane.

  1. The moment about the \(y\)-axis, \(M_y\text{,}\) is \(\ds M_y = \sum_{i=1}^n m_ix_i\text{.}\)

  2. The moment about the \(x\)-axis, \(M_x\text{,}\) is \(\ds M_x = \sum_{i=1}^n m_iy_i\text{.}\)
