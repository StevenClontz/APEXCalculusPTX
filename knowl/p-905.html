Let \(n\) be a positive integer. The following functions are continuous on their domains.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \sin(x)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \tan(x)\)

  3. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \sec(x)\)

  4. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \ln(x)\)

  5. \(f(x) = a^x\) (\(a\gt 0\))

  6. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \cos(x)\)

  7. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \cot(x)\)

  8. \(\displaystyle f(x) = \csc(x)\)

  9. \(f(x) = \sqrt[n]{x}\text{,}\) where \(n\) is a positive integer.
