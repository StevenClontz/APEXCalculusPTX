We start by considering \(f\) and take the right-hand limit of the difference quotient:

\begin{align*} \lim_{h\to0^+}\frac{f(a+h)-f(a)}{h} \amp = \lim_{h\to0^+}\frac{\sqrt{0+h}-\sqrt{0}}h\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to0^+}\frac{\sqrt{h}}h\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to0^+}\frac{1}{h^{1/2}}\ =\ \infty\text{.} \end{align*}
