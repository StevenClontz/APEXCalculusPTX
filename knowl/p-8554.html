Knowing whether or not a series converges is very important, especially when we discuss Power Series in Section 9.6. Theorems 9.2.6 and Theorem 9.2.13 give criteria for when Geometric and \(p\)-series converge, and Theorem 9.2.23 gives a quick test to determine if a series diverges. There are many important series whose convergence cannot be determined by these theorems, though, so we introduce a set of tests that allow us to handle a broad range of series. We start with the Integral Test.
