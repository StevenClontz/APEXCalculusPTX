Let \(f\) be continuous on an interval \(I\text{,}\) and differentiable on \(I\text{,}\) except possibly at \(c\text{,}\) where \(c\) is a critical number in \(I\text{.}\)

  1. If the sign of \(\fp\) switches from positive to negative at \(c\text{,}\) then \(f(c)\) is a relative maximum of \(f\text{.}\)

  2. If the sign of \(\fp\) switches from negative to positive at \(c\text{,}\) then \(f(c)\) is a relative minimum of \(f\text{.}\)

  3. If \(\fp\) is positive (or, negative) before and after \(c\text{,}\) then \(f(c)\) is not a relative extrema of \(f\text{.}\)
