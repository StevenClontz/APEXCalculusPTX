Item 1.

Consider \(y = \sin(2x)\text{.}\) Recognize that this is a composition of functions, where \(f(x) = \sin(x)\) and \(g(x) = 2x\text{.}\) Thus

\begin{align*} y' \amp = \fp(g(x))\cdot g'(x)\\ \amp = \cos(2x)\cdot \lzoo{x}{2x}\\ \amp = \cos(2x)\cdot 2\\ \amp = 2\cos(2x)\text{.} \end{align*}
