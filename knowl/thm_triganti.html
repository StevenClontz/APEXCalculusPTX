Theorem 6.1.14. Antiderivatives of Trigonometric Functions.

  1. \(\int \sin(x) \, dx = -\cos(x) +C\text{,}\)

  2. \(\int \cos(x) \, dx = \sin(x) + C\text{,}\)

  3. \(\int \tan(x) \, dx = -\ln\abs{\cos(x) }+C\text{,}\)

  4. \(\int \csc(x) \, dx = -\ln\abs{\csc(x) +\cot(x) } +C\text{,}\)

  5. \(\int \sec(x) \, dx = \ln\abs{\sec(x) +\tan(x) } + C\text{,}\)

  6. \(\int \cot(x) \, dx = \ln\abs{\sin(x) }+C\text{,}\)
