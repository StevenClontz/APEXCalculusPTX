Let \(u\) and \(v\) be differentiable functions of \(x\) on an interval \(I\) containing \(a\) and \(b\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \int u\, dv = uv - \int v\, du\text{,} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \int_{x=a}^{x=b} u\, dv = uv\Big|_a^b - \int_{x=a}^{x=b}v\, du\text{.} \end{equation*}
