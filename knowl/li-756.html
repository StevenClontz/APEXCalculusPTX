Interval 3: \((0,1)\)

We do a similar sign analysis as before, using \(p\) in \((0,1)\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \fp(p) = \frac{8}{3} \cdot \underbrace{p^{-\frac{1}{3}}}_{\gt 0}\cdot \underbrace{(p-1)}_{\lt 0}\underbrace{(p+1)}_{\gt 0}\text{.} \end{equation*}

We have two positive factors and one negative factor; \(\fp(p)\lt 0\) and so \(f\) is decreasing on \((0,1)\text{.}\)
