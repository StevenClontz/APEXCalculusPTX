This is the Alternating Harmonic Series as seen previously. The underlying sequence is \(\{a_n\} = \{1/n\}\text{,}\) which is positive, decreasing, and approaches 0 as \(n\to\infty\text{.}\) Therefore we can apply the Alternating Series Test and conclude this series converges. While the test does not state what the series converges to, we will see later that \(\ds \infser (-1)^{n+1}\frac1n=\ln(2)\text{.}\)
