Note: The second derivative notation could be written as

\begin{equation*} \frac{d^2y}{dx^2}=\frac{d^2y}{(dx)^2}=\frac{d^2}{(dx)^2}\big(y\big)\text{.} \end{equation*}

That is, we take the derivative of \(y\) twice (hence \(d^2\)), both times with respect to \(x\) (hence \((dx)^2=dx^2\)).
