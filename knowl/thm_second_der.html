Theorem 3.4.23. The Second Derivative Test.

Let \(c\) be a critical value of \(f\) where \(\fpp(c)\) is defined.

  1. If \(\fpp(c)\gt 0\text{,}\) then \(f\) has a local minimum at \((c,f(c))\text{.}\)

  2. If \(\fpp(c)\lt 0\text{,}\) then \(f\) has a local maximum at \((c,f(c))\text{.}\)
