Let \(\surfaceS_1\) be the “top” face of the cube, which can be parametrized by \(\vec r(u,v) = \langle u,v,a\rangle\) for \(-a\leq u\leq a\text{,}\) \(-a\leq v\leq a\text{.}\) We leave it to the reader to confirm that \(\vec r_u\times \vec r_v = \langle 0,0,1\rangle\text{,}\) which points outside of the cube.
