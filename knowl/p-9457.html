In Key Idea 9.8.11 (on the following page) we give a table of the Taylor series of a number of common functions. We then give a theorem about the “algebra of power series,” that is, how we can combine power series to create power series of new functions. This allows us to find the Taylor series of functions like \(f(x) = e^x\cos(x)\) by knowing the Taylor series of \(e^x\) and \(\cos(x)\text{.}\)
