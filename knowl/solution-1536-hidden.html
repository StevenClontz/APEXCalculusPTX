We compute the divergence and curl of each field following the definitions.

  1. \begin{align*} \divv \vec F \amp = \nabla \cdot \vec F = M_x+N_y+P_z = 2x+0+0= 2x\\ \curl\vec F \amp = \nabla \times \vec F = \langle P_y-N_z,M_z-P_x,N_x - M_y\rangle\\ \amp = \langle 1 - (-1), 1-1,-1-(1)\rangle = \langle 2,0,-2\rangle\text{.} \end{align*}

    For this particular field, no matter the location in space, a spin is induced with axis parallel to \(\langle 2,0,-2\rangle\text{.}\)

  2. \begin{align*} \divv \vec F \amp = \nabla \cdot \vec F = M_x+N_y+P_z = ye^{xy}+0+0= ye^{xy}\\ \curl\vec F \amp = \nabla \times \vec F = \langle P_y-N_z,M_z-P_x,N_x - M_y\rangle\\ \amp = \langle 1-\cos(x+z), -2x, \cos(x+z) - xe^{xy}\rangle \end{align*}
