Exercise 12.

Let \(\vec u = \la 1,1,-1\ra\) and \(\vec v= \la 2,1,2\ra\text{.}\)

  1. Find \(\vec u+\vec v\text{,}\) \(\vec u-\vec v\text{,}\) \(\pi\vec u-\sqrt{2}\vec v\text{.}\)

  2. Sketch the above vectors on the same axes, along with \(\vec u\) and \(\vec v\text{.}\)

  3. Find \(\vec x\) where \(\vec u+\vec x = \vec v+2\vec x\text{.}\)
