Theorem 9.2.6. Geometric Series Test.

Consider the geometric series \(\ds \infser[0] r^n\text{.}\)

  1. The \(n\)th partial sum is: \(\ds S_n = \frac{1-r^{n+1}}{1-r}\text{.}\)

  2. The series converges if, and only if, \(\abs{r} \lt 1\text{.}\) When \(\abs{r}\lt 1\text{,}\)

    \begin{equation*} \infser[0] r^n = \frac{1}{1-r}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Proof.
