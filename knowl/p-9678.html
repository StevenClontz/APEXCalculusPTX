The two foci lie on the transverse axis of the hyperbola; the midpoint of the line segment joining the foci is the center of the hyperbola. The transverse axis intersects the hyperbola at two points, each a vertex of the hyperbola. The line through the center and perpendicular to the transverse axis is the conjugate axis.
