To make our use of the Product Rule explicit, let's set \(f(x) = 5x^2\) and \(g(x) = \sin(x)\text{.}\) We easily compute/recall that \(\fp(x) = 10x\) and \(g'(x) = \cos(x)\text{.}\) Employing the rule, we have

\begin{align*} \lzoo{x}{5x^2\sin(x)} \amp= \lzoo{x}{5x^2}\sin(x) + 5x^2\lzoo{x}{\sin(x)}\\ \amp= 10x\sin(x) + 5x^2\cos(x)\text{.} \end{align*}

At \(x=\pi/2\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} y'(\pi/2) = 10\cdot\frac{\pi}2 \sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right) + 5\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)^2\cos\left(\frac{\pi}2\right) = 5\pi\text{.} \end{equation*}
