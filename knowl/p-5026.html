The Midpoint Rule averages the inputs of each subinterval to create a rectangle with height \(f\left(\frac{x_{i-1}+x_{i}}{2}\right)\text{.}\) Generally \(f\left(\frac{x_{i-1}+x_{i}}{2}\right)\neq \frac{f(x_{i-1})+f(x_{i})}{2}\text{.}\)
