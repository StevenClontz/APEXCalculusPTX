Now take the derivative of both sides of Equation (4.2.1) using implicit differentiation:

\begin{align*} \tan(\theta) \amp = \frac{x}{10}\\ \lzoo{t}{\tan(\theta)} \amp = \lzoo{t}{\frac{x}{10}}\\ \sec^2(\theta)\lz{\theta}{t} \amp = \frac{1}{10}\lz{x}{t} \end{align*}

Now we solve for \(\lz{\theta}{t}\text{:}\)

\begin{equation} \lz{\theta}{t} = \frac{\cos^2(\theta)}{10}\lz{x}{t}\tag{4.2.2} \end{equation}
